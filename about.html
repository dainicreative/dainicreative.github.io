About Me

My name is Daini Eades (Liu)

I  have about 1.5 years of experience studying and working as a User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI) Designer who combines user research, strategy, design system, prototyping, interaction design, and usability testing to transform complex abstract problems into digital products that solves business and users' problems.

Currently working at LexisNexis as a UX and Visual Designer, primarily working on Lexis+, the all-in-one ecosystem of legal solutions, from research, to data-driven insights, and practical guidance for legal professionals.

I graduated in December 2020 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Biology and Studio Art (concentrations in Graphic Design and Photography) from Wheaton College, IL, a Christian liberal arts college. 

To pursue my dream career, on top of my school work, I did a year long UX/UI design bootcamp Springboard. Then I gained some real-world experience working for two startups. 

I'm very grateful for all the people who had informational interviews with me, mentored me, offered advice and encouragement. So I'd love to pay it forward. Feel free to reach out to me to ask for virtual coffee chats about anything!

You can book a free call with me on ADPlist: https://adplist.org/mentors/daini-eades

UX design is NOT just making pretty screens!! 


Although I love visual design, making aesthetically pleasing interface, focusing on the look and feel of a product,
I would like to first make sure
(1) We're building the right product (e.g. Is it solving users' key problems? Have we done as much user research as we could?)
(2) The product is functional and usable (e.g. Do the information architecture and user flow make sense? Can the developers build it easily?)
