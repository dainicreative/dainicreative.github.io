About me

My name is Daini.

I am a Product/UX/UI/Interative/Visual/Graphic Designer who combines user research, strategy, design system, prototyping, interaction design, and usability testing to transform complex abstract problems into tangible products.

I just graduated in December 2020 with a Bachelor's degree in Biology and Studio Art (concentrations in Graphic Design and Photography) from Wheaton College, IL, a Christian liberal arts college. 

I love my work as a User Experience/User Interface/Product Designer because I love how it connects my artistic and scientific thinking; I love making people's life easier and more efficient by creating simple and intuitive products; I love finding out how others think differently from me; I love working on multifaceted creative problem solving; I love seeing the tangible impact of my work through usability testing; I love designing functional and aesthetically pleasing screens...

In short, I would like to use my creativity to design products that suit all aspects of the user's experience, business needs, and technical constraints.  

I hope to work for a company that values UX and believes in my ability to learn and grow, whether it’s startups or large corporations. I would like to get exposed to various industries, so I am especially interested in working for digital design agencies. 

I'm very grateful for all the people who had informational interviews with me, mentored me, offered advice and encouragement. So I'd love to pay it forward. Feel free to reach out to me to ask for virtual coffee chats about anything!
